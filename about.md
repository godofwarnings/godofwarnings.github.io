<h1>Hey, I'm Radhikesh.</h1>
<p class="about-sub">CS Undergraduate · IIIT Hyderabad · Making things</p>

Final-year student doing a dual degree in Computer Science (B.Tech) and
Computational Natural Sciences (M.S. by Research). Mostly I write code,
read things, and then write about both.

My current research is on foundation models for particle physics —
specifically, applying self-supervised learning to learn representations
of particle jets at the LHC. A paper on this was accepted at the ICLR
2025 ML for Physical Sciences workshop.

This blog is not a "personal brand." It's a notebook. Some posts are
technically rigorous. Some are me documenting a dream at 7am before I
forget it. Some are puzzle deep-dives. The unifying thread is that I
found it interesting enough to write down.
