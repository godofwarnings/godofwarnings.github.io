About Me

I'm a final-year undergraduate and research student at IIIT Hyderabad, pursuing a dual degree in Computer Science (B.Tech) and Computational Natural Sciences (M.S. by Research). My journey so far has been a blend of coding, research, and a bit of everything in between.

Currently, my research revolves around building foundation models for particle physics, with a focus on data from the Large Hadron Collider. One of my recent works was accepted at the ICLR 2025 Workshop, and I’m now diving deeper into this direction to uncover more insights at the core of high-energy physics.

Over the past few years, I’ve had the opportunity to explore a wide range of projects — from developing interactive geospatial platforms in collaboration with the Swedish Research Council, to creating real-time drone visualization systems using MQTT, OpenLayers, and Pixhawk telemetry. I’ve also built a custom C shell and a distributed file system inspired by Hadoop. I enjoy getting my hands dirty with systems work and learning something new with every challenge I take on.

Outside this world of (mostly enjoyable) madness, I find myself lost in another — filled with music, guitars, books, gaming (there are a few easter eggs throughout the website) and the occasional deep dive into a curiosity rabbit hole. Whether it’s research or recreation, I’m always exploring with intent and passion.

What I Do

Research & Particle Physics

  • Applying Self-Supervised Learning (e.g., JEPA) for Foundation Models in High Energy Physics.
  • Expertise in data analysis, simulation, and statistical modeling for particle jet phenomena at the LHC.
  • Proficient in developing and evaluating deep learning architectures for physics research.

Web & Full Stack Development

  • Skilled in building responsive UIs with React, HTML5, and CSS3 for interactive applications.
  • Experienced in Node.js for backend development, API design, and real-time data handling (e.g., MQTT).
  • Proficient with mapping libraries like OpenLayers for geospatial data visualization and analysis.
  • Familiar with database integration (SQL/NoSQL) and containerization using Docker.

Systems Programming & Distributed Systems

  • Proficient in C/C++ for low-level systems programming, including shell development and OS concepts.
  • Strong understanding of networking protocols, concurrency control, and building distributed file systems.

AI/ML Engineering & NLP
