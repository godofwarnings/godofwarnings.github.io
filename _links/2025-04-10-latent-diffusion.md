The paper behind Stable Diffusion. The key idea is doing diffusion in
a compressed *latent* space rather than pixel space — makes it orders
of magnitude cheaper to train and run.

Worth reading even if you don't care about image generation, because
the encoder/decoder architecture is genuinely elegant and reusable.
